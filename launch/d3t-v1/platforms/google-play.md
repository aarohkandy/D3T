# Google Play

- status: deferred
- url: https://play.google.com/console/
- accepts: Android App Bundle, not current web-only V1
- assets: 512x512 icon, 1024x500 feature graphic, phone/tablet screenshots, YouTube promo video
- sdk: native Android wrapper required before submission
- metadata: app name, short/full description, category, tags, content rating, target audience, data safety, privacy URL
- revenue: Google Play store split
- blockers: Play Console account, Android build, closed testing requirements, data safety form
- gotchas: new apps need testing tracks and tester requirements before production

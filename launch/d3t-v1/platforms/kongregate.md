# Kongregate

- status: ready after ZIP
- url: https://www.kongregate.com/games/upload
- accepts: HTML5 ZIP
- assets: screenshots, thumbnail, optional video
- sdk: optional Kongregate API; not required for V1
- metadata: title, description, instructions, category, tags
- revenue: ad revenue share
- blockers: final ZIP and images
- gotchas: lower priority due traffic decline, but simple to submit

# Press Outlets

- status: ready after trailer/GIF link
- accepts: short email pitch, press kit URL, GIF/trailer, playable link
- assets: 30s trailer or GIF, screenshots, `/presskit`
- metadata: one-line hook, platform availability, launch timing, contact email
- target outlets: Rock Paper Shotgun, PC Gamer, IndieGames+, ABG, GameDiscoverCo, relevant indie newsletters
- blockers: final contact email and trailer/GIF
- gotchas: keep pitch under 150 words and do not mass-mail without personalization
